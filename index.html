About Me

Hello! I’m Abuzar Malik, a passionate front-end developer driven by creativity and a love for building interactive web experiences. With a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I specialize in crafting visually engaging, dynamic websites that provide seamless user experiences. I’m always exploring new ways to push the boundaries of design and functionality to create sites that are both visually striking and easy to navigate.
